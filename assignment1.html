Audience

  1. Customers (Coffe drinkers and locals) come looking for information about the shop and giftcards. They generally want to see the menu, order gift cards, find the location, and occasionaly ask about updates.
  2. New Owners of Brighouse coffee and cafe seek a new website to put their brand on.

Content

  1. Icons - I will have social media icons link to their respective sites such as insta, twitter, and facebook
  2. Menu - The Menu page will be accessed from a heading bar, along with other tabs. The menu page will contain info similar to the already created menu on the Bridgehous website, but will be reformated with a more modern aperance.
  3. Gift cards - Gift cards are a common item for the shop and are frequently asked about. This Page would seek to answer the most frequently asked questions. It would also serve to give customers a means to purchase a gift card.
  4. News/ Events - The Menu often times gets updated or an event happens down town. This Page would act to present comming events and changes. Currently social media is the main source for spreading news but the current site lacks updates to current happenings.
  5. About - A simple tab/ page for information about the owners and shop its self. essentially a bio for the shop.
  6. Location - A simple section in one of the previously mentioned tabs that will display google maps location. Other informantion would also be provided based of location.
